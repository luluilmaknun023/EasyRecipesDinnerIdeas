This is an amazing dinner made in minutes that the entire family will love. This one pan Lemon Chicken Piccata Recipe has only 7 ingredients and is packed with so much flavor. This easy chicken recipe is one of our all-time favorites.

INGREDIENTS :

- 2 large boneless skinless chicken breasts cut in half length-wise.
- 1/2 teaspoon salt.
- 1/4 teaspoon pepper.
- 1/2 cup all-purpose flour.
- 1 tablespoon olive oil.
- 6 tablespoons unsalted butter divided.
- 1 cup white wine OR chicken stock.
- 3 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice.
- 4 tablespoons capers drained.

INSTRUCTIONS :

1.Sprinkle both sides of each chicken breast liberally with salt and pepper.
2. Place flour in a shallow bowl and dip each chicken breast to coat. Coat both sides, and shake off any excess. Set aside.
3. Heat a large skillet with olive oil and 3 tablespoons butter over medium/high heat.
4. Once butter is melted and butter/oil begin to shimmer/crackle, add the chicken.
5. Brown the chicken for 3-4 minutes per side, until the internal temperature reaching 165F. Remove chicken from skillet and set aside on a foil tented plate.
6. Add the chicken stock, lemon juice, and capers to the pan and lightly scrape the bottom of the pan to deglaze.
7. Add the remaining 3 tablespoons butter and allow to melt. Reduce to a simmer and allow to cook for 5 minutes to slightly thicken.
8. Return chicken to the skillet and baste in the sauce. Allow to simmer until chicken is heated through.
9. Serve drizzled with sauce. Enjoy.
